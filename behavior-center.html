POSITIVE GUIDANCE

Helping Children Learn And Grow

Positive guidance focuses on teaching children what they can do, supporting emotional development, and creating environments where children feel safe and valued.

🤝 Relationships First

Strong relationships create trust, belonging, and security. Children learn best when they feel connected to caring adults.

🧩 Supportive Environments

Predictable routines, clear expectations, and engaging spaces help children feel confident and successful.

😊 Emotional Development

Help children identify feelings, communicate needs, and develop strategies for self regulation.

📚 Teaching Expectations

Children need adults to model, practice, and teach appropriate behaviors through everyday experiences.

CLASSROOM SUPPORTS

Creating A Positive Learning Environment

A supportive classroom environment helps children understand expectations, feel secure, and participate successfully in learning experiences.

📅 Predictable Routines

Consistent routines help children know what to expect and build confidence throughout the day.

🔄 Transition Supports

Visual reminders, songs, and intentional transition strategies help children move successfully between activities.

🧩 Calm Down Spaces

Create safe areas where children can practice calming strategies and emotional regulation.

⭐ Classroom Expectations

Teach expectations through modeling, practice, encouragement, and positive reinforcement.

INTENTIONAL TEACHING PRACTICES

Supporting Children Through Purposeful Guidance

Teachers support behavior best when they combine observation, relationships, classroom structure, and intentional teaching strategies.

👀 Observe Before Responding

Take time to understand what children are communicating before choosing a response or strategy.

❤️ Build Strong Relationships

Positive relationships provide children with security, trust, and confidence to learn new skills.

📝 Document Learning

Use observations and documentation to recognize patterns and plan meaningful supports.

🌱 Teach Replacement Skills

Help children learn new ways to communicate, solve problems, and manage emotions.

FAMILY PARTNERSHIPS

Working Together To Support Children

Children thrive when families and teachers work together to understand behavior, celebrate growth, and support social emotional development.

🏡 Shared Strategies

POSITIVE BEHAVIOR SUPPORT

Children Grow Through Connection And Support

Supporting children's behavior is about more than correcting actions. It is about understanding children's development, building trusting relationships, teaching important skills, and creating environments where every child feels valued and capable.

When teachers observe with compassion and respond intentionally, children learn confidence, communication, self regulation, and positive ways to interact with others.
