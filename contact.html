Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the questions we receive most often.

Are your lesson plans free?

Many lesson plans, classroom activities, and printables are completely free. Additional premium resources and curriculum collections may also be available.

Who are your resources designed for?

Our resources are created for Early Head Start, Head Start, Preschool, and Pre Kindergarten educators working in classrooms, childcare centers, and family child care programs.

Can I use your materials in my classroom?

Absolutely. Our resources are designed to support classroom instruction and enrich children's daily learning experiences.

Do you add new resources?

Yes. We regularly publish new lesson plans, printable activities, curriculum studies, teaching tips, and seasonal classroom ideas.

Can I request a new curriculum study?

Yes. We welcome suggestions from educators. If there is a topic your classroom would enjoy, we'd love to hear your ideas.

How quickly do you respond?

We do our best to respond to every message within two business days.

Can you create customized classroom resources?

Absolutely. We can create customized lesson plans, classroom labels, newsletters, certificates, bulletin board materials, teacher forms, printables, and many other classroom resources designed specifically for your program or classroom.
