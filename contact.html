Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Common Questions

We're here to make finding educational resources simple and enjoyable. Below are answers to some of the questions we receive most often from educators, parents, and childcare professionals.

Are your educational resources free?

Many resources throughout Little Explorers Learning Hub are available at no cost. Premium curriculum studies, printable collections, and specialized resources help support the continued creation of new educational content.

Can I request a custom resource?

Absolutely! We enjoy creating personalized lesson plans, classroom printables, bulletin board materials, parent communication resources, organizational tools, and much more.

Which age groups do you support?

Our resources are designed for educators working with infants, toddlers, preschool, and Pre Kindergarten learners while supporting developmentally appropriate practices.

Do your resources support Head Start programs?

Yes. Many of our materials are created with Early Head Start and Head Start educators in mind while supporting developmentally appropriate learning experiences.

How long does it take to receive a response?

We typically respond within one to two business days. During busy periods, response times may be slightly longer, but every message receives our attention.

Can schools or organizations partner with Little Explorers Learning Hub?

Yes. We welcome collaboration with schools, childcare centers, educational organizations, curriculum developers, and fellow educators who share our passion for early childhood education.

I can't find the resource I'm looking for. What should I do?

Send us a message. If we don't already have the resource available, we'll gladly consider creating one to help meet your classroom needs.

How can I stay informed about new resources?

Subscribe to our newsletter to receive updates about new curriculum studies, lesson plans, classroom printables, teaching ideas, and professional development resources.
