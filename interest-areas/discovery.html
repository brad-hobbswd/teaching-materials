🔬 Science & STEM

Children become scientists as they investigate, explore, test ideas, and discover how the world works through hands on learning experiences.

🔬 Investigation 🧪 Experiments ⚙️ Engineering 🌎 Discovery

Explore Science Through Discovery

Children develop curiosity, observation skills, and scientific thinking through meaningful investigations and hands on experiences.

🌱

Living Things

Children observe plants, animals, insects, and living systems while learning how things grow, change, and interact.

💧

Matter & Materials

Children investigate properties of objects by exploring textures, weight, floating, sinking, mixing, and changing materials.

🧲

Forces & Motion

Children explore movement, balance, gravity, pushes, pulls, and simple machines through play and experimentation.

🌎

Earth & Environment

Children discover weather, nature, seasons, recycling, and how people interact with the world around them.

🔍

Observation Skills

Children use tools, ask questions, make predictions, and record discoveries like real scientists.

⚙️

Engineering Design

Children design, build, test, and improve creations while developing problem solving skills.

Hands On Science Investigations

Children learn science by asking questions, making predictions, testing ideas, and exploring the world around them.

💧

Sink or Float

Children test different objects, make predictions, and discover why some materials sink while others float.

🔍

Magnifying Glass Explorers

Children observe small details in nature, examine textures, and discover patterns they may not notice with their eyes alone.

🌱

Plant Investigations

Children observe plant growth, compare changes, and learn how living things need care to grow.

🐞

Garden Creature Discovery

Children investigate insects and small animals while learning how living things interact within an environment.

🌦️

Weather Watchers

Children observe weather patterns, track changes, and discuss how weather affects people, plants, and animals.

🎨

Color Science

Children mix colors, observe changes, and explore how different colors are created through experimentation.

🧲

Magnet Discovery

Children explore magnetic and non magnetic objects while developing prediction and problem solving skills.

🌑

Light and Shadows

Children investigate light sources, shadows, and how objects change when light moves.

STEM Challenges & Engineering Experiences

Children become problem solvers as they design, build, test, and improve their own creations.

🏗️

Build a Bridge

Children use blocks, craft materials, and recycled items to design bridges that support weight and solve building challenges.

🚗

Create a Ramp

Children explore motion by designing ramps and testing how objects move, roll, and travel different distances.

🏠

Design a Shelter

Children create structures that protect people, animals, or classroom objects while exploring design and materials.

🛠️

Invent a Tool

Children identify problems and create tools or inventions that help make tasks easier.

♻️

Recycled Engineering

Children reuse materials to build new creations while learning about sustainability and creative problem solving.

💡

Solve a Problem

Children work together to ask questions, test ideas, and develop solutions through teamwork and creativity.

Creating a Science Discovery Environment

A well-designed science area encourages children to observe, investigate, experiment, and develop a lifelong curiosity about the world.

🔍

Observation Tools

Provide magnifying glasses, measuring tools, clipboards, and materials that encourage children to look closely and record discoveries.

🧪

Experiment Area

Children explore simple experiments using safe materials while learning about cause and effect, prediction, and change.

🌱

Living Things

Plants, insects, and nature collections help children observe growth, life cycles, and connections in the environment.

📓

Science Journals

Children draw pictures, record observations, make predictions, and communicate their scientific thinking.

⚙️

Building Materials

Blocks, recycled materials, and loose parts encourage engineering, creativity, and problem solving.

🌎

Nature Exploration

Children investigate rocks, leaves, weather, animals, and natural materials through hands on experiences.

Families can continue science learning beyond the classroom by exploring nature walks, cooking experiments, gardening, weather observations, building projects, and everyday discoveries together. Simple conversations and hands on experiences help children develop curiosity and confidence as young scientists.
