🍞 Bread Study

Mix, measure, knead, bake, taste, and discover how bread is made around the world through hands-on cooking experiences, science investigations, creativity, and cultural exploration.

FOOD • SCIENCE • CULTURE

Discovering Bread Through Exploration

Children explore the process of making bread through hands-on experiences with measuring, mixing, kneading, baking, and tasting. Through this study, children investigate science, mathematics, literacy, culture, and creativity while discovering how bread connects people around the world.

HANDS-ON FOOD SCIENCE

Classroom Investigations

Children explore how ingredients change through mixing, measuring, kneading, baking, and tasting experiences.

🥣 Mixing Ingredients

Children combine ingredients and observe how separate materials become a mixture.

📏 Measuring & Recipes

Children use measuring tools to follow simple recipes and explore quantities.

🤲 Kneading Dough

Children explore texture, movement, and how dough changes through hands-on investigation.

🔥 Baking Changes

Children observe how heat transforms dough into a finished loaf of bread.

🌎 Bread Around The World

Children discover different breads, traditions, and food experiences from cultures around the world.

😋 Taste Testing

Children describe flavors, textures, and preferences while practicing communication skills.

INTEREST AREAS

Bread Learning Centers

🔬 Science Area

Explore ingredients, dough changes, yeast, textures, and cooking observations.

🍳 Dramatic Play

Create bakeries, restaurants, and pretend kitchens while exploring food experiences.

🎨 Art Area

Create bread-inspired artwork using textures, colors, and creative materials.

📚 Literacy Area

Read cooking books, explore recipes, and practice new food vocabulary.

🧮 Math Area

Count ingredients, measure amounts, compare sizes, and follow sequences.

🌾 Sensory Area

Explore flour, grains, dough textures, and safe sensory materials.
