📦 Boxes Study

Children explore the world of cardboard boxes through engineering, creativity, problem solving, and imaginative play. Children investigate size, shape, structure, and design while transforming ordinary boxes into meaningful creations.

ENGINEERING • STEM • CREATIVITY

Engineering Through Boxes & Design

Cardboard boxes provide endless opportunities for children to become designers, builders, and problem solvers. Through creating structures, vehicles, habitats, and imaginative creations, children explore early engineering concepts while developing creativity and collaboration.

Children use imagination to transform boxes into meaningful projects.

HANDS-ON ENGINEERING

Classroom Investigations

Children explore how boxes can be transformed through creativity, design, testing, and problem solving.

📦 Box Transformations

Children investigate how a simple box can become something new such as a house, vehicle, robot, or imaginative creation.

🏗 Building Structures

Children stack, connect, and balance boxes while discovering what makes structures strong and stable.

📏 Design & Measure

Children compare box sizes, measure materials, and plan designs before building.

💡 Engineering Challenges

Children solve design problems by creating, testing, changing, and improving their inventions.

INTEREST AREAS

Boxes Learning Centers

🏗 Block Area

Children build towers, buildings, bridges, and structures using different sizes of boxes.

🎨 Art Area

Children decorate boxes and transform them into creative projects using various materials.

🔬 Science Area

Children explore balance, weight, materials, and how structures work.

📚 Literacy Area

Children read stories about building, imagination, inventions, and design.

🧮 Math Area

Children measure, compare, sort, and count boxes by size and shape.

🏠 Dramatic Play

Children create pretend spaces such as stores, homes, vehicles, and community buildings.
