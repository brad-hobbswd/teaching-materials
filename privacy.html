Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Little Explorers Learning Hub collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website.

Last Updated

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 1, 2026 and may be updated periodically as our website and services evolve.



Information We Collect

Depending on how you use our website, we may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Messages submitted through our contact form
  • Newsletter subscriptions
  • Information you choose to provide when contacting us


How We Use Your Information

Information may be used to:

  • Respond to questions and requests.
  • Deliver newsletters and educational updates.
  • Improve our website and teaching resources.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Maintain website security.


Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, remember preferences, and analyze website performance. You may disable cookies within your browser settings if you choose.



Third Party Services

We may use trusted third party services such as email providers, analytics platforms, embedded videos, or payment processors. These services maintain their own privacy policies and security practices.

Children's Privacy

Little Explorers Learning Hub is designed as a resource for educators, caregivers, and families. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our website, please contact us so we can promptly remove the information.



How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to help protect the personal information you provide. While no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, we work to safeguard your information and continuously improve our security practices.



Your Choices

You may choose to:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time.
  • Request updates or corrections to your information.
  • Request deletion of personal information you have shared with us, when applicable.
  • Disable cookies through your web browser settings.


External Links

Our website may include links to other educational websites, organizations, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing personal information.



Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to our website, services, or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page along with the revised effective date.



Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us through our Contact page.
