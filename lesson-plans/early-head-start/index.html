Learning Begins With Relationships

Early Head Start classrooms provide safe, nurturing environments where infants and toddlers learn through responsive interactions, exploration, movement, sensory experiences, and everyday routines. Lesson plans are flexible, individualized, and built around children's interests while supporting the Early Learning Outcomes Framework.

Sensory Experiences

🌿

Nature Exploration

Texture Discovery

🎨

Sensory Art

🫧
