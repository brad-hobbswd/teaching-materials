Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our printable resources, downloads, and classroom materials.

Are the printable resources free?

Many printable resources are available free of charge, while larger collections and specialty resource bundles may be offered as premium downloads. Each resource clearly indicates whether it is free or part of a bundle.

What age groups are the resources designed for?

Resources are created for Early Head Start, Head Start, Preschool, and Pre Kindergarten classrooms. Many activities can also be adapted for kindergarten or home learning environments.

Can I use these resources in my classroom?

Yes. Our printable resources are designed for use in classrooms, childcare programs, family childcare homes, and educational settings. Please review our Terms of Use for information about sharing and redistribution.

How often are new resources added?

New printables, curriculum studies, lesson plans, and classroom tools are added regularly throughout the year. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay informed about the latest additions.

Can I request a custom printable?

Absolutely. We welcome ideas from educators. If you need a personalized classroom resource, simply visit our Contact page and tell us what you're looking for.

Do you offer curriculum aligned resources?

Our materials are designed to support developmentally appropriate practices and can be used alongside many early childhood curricula and state early learning standards.
