Developmental Milestones

Children between two and three years continue making tremendous progress in every area of development through meaningful experiences and responsive relationships.

🗣️ Uses longer sentences and communicates wants, needs, and ideas.
📚 Begins recognizing favorite books, songs, and familiar stories.
👫 Plays alongside and with other children during classroom activities.
🧩 Solves simple problems through exploration and trial and error.
✂️ Improves cutting, drawing, painting, and fine motor control.
🏃 Runs, jumps, climbs, balances, and enjoys active outdoor play.
🎭 Uses imagination during dramatic play experiences.
❤️ Builds independence through daily classroom routines.

Partnering with Families

Strong partnerships between home and school help children feel secure, confident, and successful. Encourage families to read together, sing songs, explore outdoors, talk throughout the day, and involve children in simple everyday routines like cooking, cleaning, sorting, and gardening. These shared experiences strengthen language, relationships, and lifelong learning.
