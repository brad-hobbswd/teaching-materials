Essential Questions

Open ended questions encourage children to predict, investigate, communicate, and think critically throughout each curriculum study.

How do we learn by asking questions?
What happens when we observe something closely?
How can we solve problems together?
Why do things change over time?
How do books help us learn about our world?
What patterns can we discover around us?

Family Connections

Families play an important role in preparing children for kindergarten. Encourage daily reading, writing together, counting everyday objects, exploring nature, asking questions, building with blocks, drawing pictures, and talking about new discoveries. These everyday experiences help children become confident lifelong learners.
