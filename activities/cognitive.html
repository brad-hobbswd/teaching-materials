Teacher Tips for Supporting Cognitive Development

Small changes in the classroom create powerful learning opportunities.

💬

Ask Open Questions

Encourage children to explain their thinking with questions like "Why?" and "How do you know?"

🔎

Allow Exploration

Give children time to investigate, experiment, and discover independently.

🧩

Offer Challenges

Provide activities that encourage children to think beyond the first solution.

⭐

Celebrate Thinking

Focus on children's process, effort, and creativity.
