Sensory Skills Children Develop

Sensory experiences help children explore, communicate, problem solve, and understand the world around them.

👋

Exploration Skills

Children investigate materials, notice differences, and learn through hands-on discovery.

🧠

Cognitive Development

Children compare, predict, sort, classify, and solve problems.

💬

Language Growth

Children build vocabulary by describing textures, sounds, colors, and experiences.

✋

Fine Motor Skills

Scooping, pouring, squeezing, and manipulating materials strengthen hands.

Teacher Strategies for Sensory Learning

Create meaningful sensory experiences that encourage curiosity and discovery.

🔎

Ask Open Ended Questions

Encourage children to describe, predict, and explain what they notice.

👐

Offer Different Materials

Provide a variety of textures, objects, and sensory experiences.

💬

Build Vocabulary

Use words like rough, smooth, heavy, light, loud, and quiet.

🌱

Follow Children's Interests

Allow children to explore, repeat experiences, and discover.
