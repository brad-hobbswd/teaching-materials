Teacher Strategies for Creative Learning

Create an environment where children feel free to explore and create.

🖌️

Provide Open Ended Materials

Offer loose parts, recycled materials, and art supplies that encourage creativity.

💬

Ask About Their Ideas

Encourage children to explain their creations and describe their thinking.

🎭

Encourage Imagination

Use dramatic play and storytelling to expand creative thinking.

⭐

Celebrate Process

Focus on children's creativity, effort, and exploration rather than perfection.
