Engaging At-Home Experiences

These activities are designed for families to support children’s learning at home by building on classroom exploration, curiosity, and discovery.

🌿 Nature Walk Journal

Take a walk outside and notice the changing seasons. Collect leaves, sticks, and treasures, then draw or write about your discoveries.

View Details →

📖 Story Sharing Night

Read a favorite book together. Share favorite parts or illustrate a scene to spark conversation and literacy skills.

View Details →

🧪 Kitchen Science Fun

Explore science at home using safe experiments like baking soda + vinegar, or try making ice cream in a bag together.

View Details →

🎨 Art & Expression

Create family art using crayons, paints, recycled materials, or nature objects. Discuss what each creation represents.

View Details →

🎶 Family Dance Party

Put on some music and dance together! Explore movement, rhythm, and patterns as you move—good for physical activity and joy!

View Details →

🧩 Puzzle Challenge Time

Work together on puzzles, building blocks, or sorting games. Talk about shapes, colors, patterns, and problem solving.
